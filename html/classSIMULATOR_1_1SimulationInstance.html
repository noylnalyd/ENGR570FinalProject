◆ runSim()

void SIMULATOR::SimulationInstance::runSim ( )

Run the simulation.

Performs fixed-timestep iteration until the simmodel's end condition hits or 3600s is achieved. Uses PBM's version of Gauss-Seidel solving. May be modified to produce iterative updates to the terminal.
